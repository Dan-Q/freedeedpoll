Help

FreeDeedPoll.org.uk is a free and open-source website that, since 2011, has helped tens of thousands of British citizens to change their legally-recognised name for free.

If you need help with the site or with changing your name using a free deed poll, please see the FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions).

There's also a guide for if an organisation rejects your deed poll. Most people don't need it, but if you do, it's there to help.

If you've read those page and still need help, you're welcome to email me on dan@freedeedpoll.org.uk. This address attracts a lot of spam, so please use a clear and explicit subject line that summarises the query within, e.g. "Free Deed Poll: I have dual citizenship - can I use a free deed poll?". I am not a lawyer and cannot provide legal advice, but I've helped many hundreds of people who've had difficulties changing their names and I'll do my best to help you.
