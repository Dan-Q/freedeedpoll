How to change your name for free

As a British citizen, you have a right to be known by (almost) any name you choose, by creating a deed poll.

Plenty of people will charge you for the privilege, but there's no need to pay a penny! You can join the tens of thousands of people who've changed their name for free using this website.

Here's what you need to do:

  1. First, read the checklist to make sure you have everything you need.
  2. Then, if you've got any questions, see if they've been answered already.
  3. Next, fill out a form to create your deed poll.
  4. Print it out, sign it, and send copies of it to [the relevant places]. You don't need to wait, though: you can start using your new name right away!
