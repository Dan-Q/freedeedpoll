Eligibility

If you're a British citizen, you have a right to be known by (almost) any name you choose. A deed poll is simply a way of showing that you're serious about your new name and can make it easier to persuade large companies (like your bank) and government agencies (like the Passport Office) to update their records.

It's possible to pay somebody to write you out a deed poll, or to pay a solicitor to put a stamp on it, but in the vast majority of cases this is an unnecessary expense, not to mention additional work on your part. Using this website, you can produce a valid, widely-accepted deed poll for free and with minimal effort.

You should read the rest of this page carefully before you start: changing your name is a serious undertaking with real consequences and you ought to familiarise yourself with the procedure before you start.
