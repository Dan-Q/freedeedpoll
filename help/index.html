Help

FreeDeedPoll.org.uk is a free and open-source website that, since 2011, has helped tens of thousands of British citizens to change their legally-recognised name for free.

If you need help with the site or with changing your name using a free deed poll, please see the FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions).

If you've read the FAQ and still need help, you're welcome to email me on freedeedpoll@danq.me. This address attracts a lot of spam, so please use a clear and explicit subject line that summarises the query within, e.g. "Free Deed Poll: I have dual citizenship - can I use a free deed poll?". I am not a lawyer and cannot provide legal advice, but I've helped many hundreds of people who've had difficulties changing their names and I'll do my best to help you.
